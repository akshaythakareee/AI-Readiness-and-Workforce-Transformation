## 📌 Project Overview

This Tableau project analyzes how **AI automation is reshaping the workforce** — identifying which job roles are most vulnerable, how different departments are adapting, and what strategic actions organizations should take to stay ahead.

Built as a **multi-dashboard Tableau Story**, the project goes from raw workforce data to **executive-ready strategic recommendations** using visual storytelling.

### Story — Strategic Recommendations for AI-Ready Workforce

> *From data to decisions.*

<img width="1920" height="1080" alt="Screenshot (347)" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/65d29ef7-5bf3-4c12-83e8-50208711add5" />

**Key Insights:**
- Several job roles face high automation risk and require reskilling
- Some departments show lower AI adoption compared to automation exposure
- Workforce transition is occurring through augmentation and role transformation

**Recommended Actions:**
- 🎯 Launch reskilling & AI training programs for high-risk roles
- 📢 Increase AI adoption initiatives in lagging departments
- 🤝 Focus on augmenting employees with AI tools rather than replacing them
- 🗺️ Build a long-term AI capability development strategy
